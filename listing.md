## Full Description

### Know exactly how long your money lasts — and when to start fundraising.

The ClearMetric Startup Runway Calculator is a **professional-grade Excel spreadsheet** that helps founders:

- **Calculate runway** — months until cash runs out at current burn and revenue
- **Model planned hires** — see how new team members affect runway
- **Plan fundraising** — when to start (6 months before runway ends), target raise, close month
- **Get the Default Alive verdict** — Paul Graham's framework: will you reach profitability?
- **Find break-even** — when revenue first exceeds expenses

### What's Inside

**Sheet 1: Runway Calculator**
- Enter cash, revenue, growth rate, and expense categories (salaries, office, software, marketing, legal, other)
- Planned hires: number, salary, start month
- One-time expenses and fundraising (target raise, close month)
- Instantly see: total burn, net burn, runway, break-even month, critical cash threshold
- Default Alive? verdict with explanation
- Start fundraising by: 6 months before cash runs out

**Sheet 2: Monthly Projection**
- 36 months of cash, revenue, expenses, net, and cumulative net
- All formulas linked to Runway Calculator — change inputs, see updates
- Handles hires, one-time expenses, and fundraising injections

**Sheet 3: Scenario Comparison**
- Compare 3 paths side-by-side: Current, Lean (cut burn), Aggressive growth
- Edit Lean burn and Aggressive growth to test different strategies
- See runway and break-even for each scenario

**Sheet 4: How To Use**
- Quick start guide
- Runway, net burn, break-even, Default Alive explained
- Scenario comparison tips
